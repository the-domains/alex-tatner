Rise Locations

The physical location is a large part of what Rise delivers. It houses the Barclays Accelerator and provides co-working offices for entrepreneurs to run their businesses and space to run events.

The spaces are designed with the intent of the brand in mind. Giving a relaxed home office feel with subtle references to the brand throughout the space.

Rise Brand

Rise is the physical product of the Bank for Entrepreneurs strategy.

The spaces are currently open in Whitechapel, Manchester, New York and Cape town, their aim; to provide a serendipitous environment for startup businesses to co-create, connect and scale.

I was part of the team responsible for designing the global brand and digital strategy.
